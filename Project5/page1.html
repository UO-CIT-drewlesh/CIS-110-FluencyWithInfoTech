MacMiller

A Musical Master

Malcolm James McCormick, commonly known as Mac Miller, a rap star. He talks about his troubles through his music and it has touched the lives of many. Anyone that listens to his music can tell you that. His lyrics are filled with fluent wordplay and beats that has made him a huge role in the musical world.

Early Life

Mac was born on January 19th, 1992. From a young age, he was driven by music. Learning many instruments and singing/rapping. By high school he knew he wanted to be a creative genius. And that it what he did.

Albums

His first album was released in 2007 by the name of Easy Mac. Although he had a breakthrough when K.I.D.S. was presented in 2010. Since then, he released 9 more albums that all rose him up and up.

Death

September 7th, 2018. Mac Miller was pronounced dead due to a drug overdose. His songs talk so much about his struggles, that seeing something he couldn't overcome take him down was and still is heartbreaking. Rest In Peace to Mac Miller. A legend that touched the hearts of so many people.
